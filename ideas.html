A Page for My Wild Ideas

  • OpenSpace

    Spacerace 2.0 has commenced with the advent of privatized space companies like SpaceX ushering in the new age with reuseable rocket technology. This opens up the outer levels of space as a new frontier for commerce and civilization at large. Who will have the means to take advantage of first access? Initially and speculatively for more than decades to come, only the highly privileged will be able. How much more obvious can the gap between the super rich and the poor be portrayed than its stratospheric manifestation? Time and time again, the collective might of the open community has come together to release hoarded power from the one or few as to benefit the rest of humanity. With the enthusiasm and crowd source of the makers, doers, and FOSS community, could the collective once again be able to provide a solution as to make space open not for just the privileged few but for all?
